Lets make student life Easier ...!

College Intellibot project is built using artificial algorithms that analyzes user’s queries and understand user’s message.

This System is a web application which provides answer to the query of the student.

Students just have to query through the bot which is used for chatting.

College Info Intellibot is very useful for students,faculties as well as Parents since :

  • They does not have to go personally to college office for the enquiry.

  • This web-application enables the students to be updated with college cultural activities.

  • This web-application saves time for the student as well as teaching and non-teaching staffs.



The vision of college intellibot is to make students life easier.

College Intellibot answers all the queries of the student in the simplest way.

The functionalities provided by the college intellibot will help the students to search about -

  • College Events:They could get the information about all the college events from here.

  • Study Materials:College Intellibot helps student to get best study material present for any subject and topic they want.

  • Syllabus:Students could check old as well as latest syllabus of subjects from here.

  • Datesheet:It also provides datesheets for exams and holidays.

  • Results:You can check your semester results directly from here.

  • Placements:Students could get information about upcoming drives.

  • Important Contacts:Students could get all important contacts of college from here and also could contact them directly from the bot.



College Intellibot is not only helpful for the students but also for Parents.

They can easily get the informations without actually going to college.

College Intellibot provides feature to pay fees directly from the app and also contains the fee structure of all years and branches.

They does not have to go personally to college office for the enquiry.

Parents could also check the result of their child from here.



Admin is the person who will help you whenever you are in trouble

Admin is available 24*7 to help user.

User could easily contact admin just by typing 'admin' and admin will be available to you in no time.

This feature of live chat with admin makes this application so impressive and user friendly.

Admin updates all the data of the college Intellibot.

User could ask admin to add new functionalities, report bugs, suggest improvements etc.
